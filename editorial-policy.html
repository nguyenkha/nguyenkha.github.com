Editorial Policy

Last updated: April 21, 2026

kha.do is a personal publication focused on firsthand writing about web engineering, fintech, blockchain, aviation, travel, and creator workflows.

How Content Is Created

Most posts are written from direct personal experience, product building, or real-world travel observation. When opinions are included, they are clearly presented as personal perspective.

Quality Standards

I prioritize original insights, practical context, and clear structure. Thin, outdated, or low-value entries may be revised, archived, or excluded from search indexing.

Corrections

If a factual error is identified, I update the post as soon as possible. Material changes may be reflected in the publication timeline.
